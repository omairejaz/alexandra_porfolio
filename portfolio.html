My Portfolio

Here you can watch my personal content for filmography and photography!

All films are Written, Directed and Edited by Alexandra Savu

Carpathian Travelogue

Date:12 November 2020

Description: This film is a Carpathian Travelogue filmed in the mountains of Romania during the summer of 2020. All the videos edited were shot on spot, while hiking and mountain-climbing, in order to give the film a more authentic and personal look.

Tears in the Sky

Date:15 March 2020

Description: This represents my first independently directed, written, and produced film in the United States. I have worked with some of my closest friends as actors and had a crew of about 3 people. I wanted to be in charge as much as possible during all stages of production, since the movie touches on a very personal matter and highlights my experiences as a foreigner in a country across the world from home.

Dallas Farmers Market

Date:7 October 2020

Description: As I am always fascinated by the skyline of big American cities, I made a short film of the stunning view from the Trinity Skyline that appears everywhere in postcards.

TravelBackHome

Date:30 August 2020

Description: On my way back home to SMU after a summer spent in Romania, airplanes and airports can become very emotional and meaningful in different ways. Thus, I documented those transitions that several international students can relate to for SMU Daily Campus.

Memory

Date:29 July 2020

Description: While I was alone on campus during the first phase of quarantine, I decided to experiment by creating a black and white film with the few people who were around. It outlines the uncertainty of life and the lack of closure or understanding in different relationships.

Mustang Band documentary

Date:14 December 2019

Description: I had the amazing opportunity to interview the SMU Mustang Band director, Don Hopkins and the dedicated band member, Natalie Igo, who helped me release this band documentary. The insights on the rehearsals, games and SMU spirit developed among band students are all accurate and aim to convey authentic and strong emotions.

A New Perspective

Date:28 September 2018

Description: This is a movie meant to defy the social constraints of how and who should show affection and/or interest. It is an experimental short film that emphasizes the power of women, which should be more valued in relationships. It was released with my high-school friends who helped with acting and casting.

Confusion

Date:10 March 2019

Description: Description: This is a movie created for the application at several film programs and secured the entry to many film workshops. I had to respect certain guidelines for the direction and screenwriting of this short film.

4 Poles 1 Line

Date:7 October 2018

Description: This is a documentary of a famous tramway in Bucharest that comprises the authentic, unconventional atmosphere of the Eastern European capital. I released it during a documentary workshop, where equipment, and technical and artistic instructions were provided.

Documentary of SMU Debate Team

Date:26 November 2019

Description: As part of the SMU Debate Team, I had the chance to make a documentary which substantially presents the life behind the scenes of these passionate and stubborn debaters, whose goal is not only to win, but also to learn the art of persuasiveness.
