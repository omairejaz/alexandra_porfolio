3008 Studio

Date: November 2020 - present

Role: Post Production Editor

Description: I am responsible for the color corrections and after effects implementation for a 28-minute horror film released by 3008 Productions. I was hired to polish the final edits and create a trailer for the short film before it was distributed through Amazon Prime. I also assisted in other processes, such as sound mixing or the distribution phase.

SMU Daily Campus

Date:May 2020 - present

Role: Video Editor & Videographer

Description: I am responsible for the visual content filmed and edited for the school newspaper and for the stories and articles chosen to be published within it. I document a large audience on the events taking place both on campus and internationally, depending on their importance to our social community. I take interviews, film B-roll and respect deadlines and templates for delivering journalistic, visually stimulating news reports.

SMU Cox Schools

Date: October 2019 - present

Role: Video Editor

Description: As the video editor for the School of Business at SMU, I deal with the recordings of the lectures taken every day. I make sure the sound is comprehensible by mastering the audio tracks, and I edit the beginnings and ending by adding transitions. I also frame and adjust the video and cut it in parts so that students and teachers can view it easily online.

SMU Student Affairs

Date:January – May 2020

Role: Video Editor & Videographer

Description: I filmed and edited interviews and B-roll clips for creating several videos for a mental health project led by the university department of student affairs. I released the documentary video during the week of awareness for mental health issues, so that college students could be more open and educated on this topic.

SMU Armstrong Commons

Date: May 2020 - present

Role: Video Editor & Videographer

Description: As the video editor for the School of Business, I deal with the recordings of the lectures taken each day. I make sure the audio is comprehensible, I edit the beginning and ending, frame and adjust the video and cut it in parts so that students and teachers can view it easily online.

European Youth Parliament

Date: February 2019

Role: Digital Media Member

Description: I worked as a media team member for an international event in Vienna, Austria for the young students interested in politics and foreign relations. I filmed and edited two videos each day, which were published on the social media pages and posted photographs daily during the event.

Arca Film Studios

Date: July - August 2018

Role: Production Assistant and Assistant Director

Description: I got involved in the production of a feature documentary, shot in Romania, and directed by a university professor of film directing, Florin Kevorkian. I worked with logistics, financial budgets and oversaw crowd control and the organization of the basecamp from the filming locations in the Carpathian Mountains. I also helped create the shot list for each scene and set design during each night of the film shooting.

SOS Children’s Villages

Date: July - August 2018

Role: Cinematography Teacher

Description: I enrolled into a volunteering program at a shelter for unprivileged children who were seeking education from exterior sources. I have led workshops about the importance of old movies to children aging between 12 and 14 years old. I have talked about the basics of filmmaking and I have introduced them to the Film Noirs, more specifically, to the movies of Hitchcock, cultivating their interest for black and white films.

Check out below the films I have worked on or I am currently working on in different stages of production!

Brunch

Release Date: December 2020

Crew position: Director of Photography

Description of film/role: I worked as the director of photography on a student short film that was shot in October 2020. I met with the director to refine the script, work through the lines of dialogue, decide the order and importance of events, and create a very detailed shot list for each scene. I worked with the FS7 and had 3 changeable lenses (25mm, 50mm, and 85 mm). The movie is about an unexpected outcome of a family brunch when the daughter brings home her supposed boyfriend.

GRIT

Release Date: December 2020

Crew position: Editor

Description of film/role: I was assigned to edit in Avid Media Composer a 10-minute short film directed by Arnulfo Garza. It portrays the struggles to decide what is the right thing to do, especially when civilians’ lives are involved. The process of editing consisted of creating several rough cuts and fine cuts until achieving the desired picture lock, and assisting the director with the post-production processes of color correction and sound mixing.

Together, Alone

Release Date: December 2020

Crew position: Sound mixer & Boom Operator

Description of film/role: The film is directed by Alexa May, a “Women In Film” scholarship-winner, and illustrates the hardship of letting our dear ones pass away when there is nothing else to say or do. The actors had lavaliers and I worked with the Marantz for sound recording.

Heart House Documentary

Release Date: May 2021

Crew position:

Sound mixer, 1st Assistant Camera, Assistant Director, Gaffer, Grip

Description of film/role: I worked on Cullen Blanchfield’s thesis film, an independent project where he documented the emotional hardships of unprivileged people during the global pandemic. I set up the lighting for his interviews, worked on sound recording, and helped him with the camera and set design. I will also contribute to the post-production phases of his documentary.
